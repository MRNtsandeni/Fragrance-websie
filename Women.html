Giorgio Armani - My Way

Giorgio Armani My Way is a floral fragrance launched in 2020, featuring notes of bergamot, orange blossom, tuberose, jasmine, cedarwood, vanilla, and white musk. It embodies a spirit of discovery and connection, appealing to modern, confident women. The perfume comes in a sustainable, refillable bottle and supports reforestation efforts. With its elegant, fresh scent, My Way is versatile for both everyday wear and special occasions.
